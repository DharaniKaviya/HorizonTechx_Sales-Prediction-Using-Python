📌 About the Project

What is Sales Prediction?

This project uses machine learning regression algorithms to forecast future sales based on advertising budgets, marketing platforms, campaign types, and regional data. It enables data-driven business decisions.

  • Analyze the impact of TV, Radio, Social & Digital advertising on revenue
  • Compare multiple ML models and automatically select the best performer
  • Generate professional EDA visualizations and business insights
  • Provide real-time sales predictions via an interactive web interface
  • Support budget optimization with ROI and efficiency scoring
